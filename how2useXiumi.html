How to publish your own wechat public account article by using Xiumi?

  1. Visit Xiumi and sign up (using your QQ, Wechat, Weibo or email).

  2. After logging in, Click 图文排版(layouts).

  3. Click 我的图文（my article) and 添加新的图文(add a new article).

  4. Now we can arrange the structure of your article.Type in the title of your article.

  5. Write an abstract of your article in the marked area with no more than 50 words for the convenience of your readers.

  6. Click 图文模板(templets) and 标题(title), you can use the provided examples and replace them with your ideas. The same is with 贴纸（decoration)、背景图（backgroud pictures)、分割线（split line) and so on.

  7. Next choose a front cover for your titile to attract the readers.

  8. Don't forget to preview and save your article.
